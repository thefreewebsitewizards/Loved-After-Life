Love Doesn't End. Neither Should the Conversation.

Loved After Life is a grief-tech service that helps you preserve and reconnect with the voice, stories, and personality of someone you love — even after they pass.

Using advanced AI voice cloning and memory-based training, we allow you to call or text a private phone number linked to your loved one's digital voice. Whether you're preparing for what's to come or holding onto what was, this is a deeply personal way to continue the bond.

This isn't a recording. It's an evolving conversation, powered by your memories — and their voice.

How It Works

Step 1: Voice Capture

We collect voice samples from:

  • Guided voice recording sessions (if the person is still alive)
  • Existing media (voicemails, home videos, YouTube, interviews, phone calls)
  • Even short clips — our technology can work with limited data

Step 2: Memory & Personality Training

We use interviews, submitted stories, personality descriptions, and life details to train the AI to speak like them — not just in sound, but in spirit. You can provide:

  • Written memories
  • Voice notes
  • Surveys about personality traits, catchphrases, beliefs, etc.
  • Favorite sayings, jokes, advice, and more

Step 3: Private Access via Phone & Text

Once your AI voice model is ready, you'll receive:

  • A private phone number you can call anytime to hear their voice
  • A text line you can message, and receive responses in their voice and tone
  • (Think of it like having their number again.)
  • Everything is secure, private, and only accessible by you.

Choose How You Want to Connect

Mode 1: Preserved Voice (Memory-Locked)

Only uses exact sayings, known stories, and approved responses from the person's life. Ideal for memorial-style connection or for those who want accuracy and boundaries.

Mode 2: Simulated Consciousness (Beta)

A more adaptive mode where the AI evolves and responds like they would have — based on personality, history, and stories. The AI can generate new responses, offer advice, even reflect over time.

This is ideal for those seeking ongoing emotional comfort or a "what they'd say" experience.

You can switch between modes or lock one in permanently.

We also support active learning, with version control — so you can update or roll back if needed.
